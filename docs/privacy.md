# Privacy Policy - ASR Health Portal

## Overview

ASR Health Portal is committed to protecting your privacy. This document explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal and health data.

## Data We Collect

### Account Information
- Email address
- Name (optional)
- Authentication tokens

### Meal Planning Data
- Dietary preferences
- Allergies and restrictions
- ZIP code (for store availability)
- Generated meal plans
- Shopping lists

### Nutrition Data
- Food logs
- Calorie tracking
- Nutritional goals

### Health Integration Data (when enabled)
- **Steps**: Daily step counts from connected apps
- **Sleep**: Sleep duration and quality metrics

### Usage Data
- Feature interactions
- Error logs
- Performance metrics

## Data We Do NOT Collect

- ❌ Medical diagnoses
- ❌ Prescription information
- ❌ Detailed health records
- ❌ Location tracking
- ❌ Heart rate or vital signs (not imported even if available)

## How We Use Your Data

### Personalization
- Customize meal recommendations
- Track your progress
- Provide relevant coaching

### Service Improvement
- Identify and fix bugs
- Improve features
- Understand usage patterns

### Never Sold
- We **never sell** your personal data
- We **never share** data with third parties for marketing

## Data Storage

### Local Storage
Most data is stored locally on your device:
- Meal plan preferences
- Nutrition logs
- Habit tracking
- Imported health data
- Feature settings

### Server Storage
Limited data stored on servers:
- Account credentials (encrypted)
- Subscription status
- Generated meal plans (for retrieval)

### Token Security
For health integrations:
- OAuth tokens obfuscated before storage
- Tokens scoped to your user account
- Automatic expiration checking
- No plaintext storage

## Your Rights

### Access Your Data
You can view all stored data in your browser's developer tools:
- Open DevTools (F12)
- Go to Application > Local Storage
- View `asr_*` prefixed items

### Delete Your Data
You can delete your data at any time:
1. **Health Data**: Use "Disconnect" in Integrations
2. **Account Data**: Contact support for full deletion
3. **Local Data**: Clear browser storage

### Export Your Data
Contact support to request a data export.

### Opt Out
- Feature flags control optional features
- Disable integrations anytime
- Delete account to remove all data

## Data Security

### Encryption
- All API communications use HTTPS
- Passwords hashed with bcrypt
- OAuth tokens obfuscated

### Access Control
- Token-based authentication
- Session expiration
- User-scoped data access

### No Cross-User Access
- Data keyed by user ID
- No shared data between accounts
- Isolation enforced at storage level

## Third-Party Integrations

### Health Apps
When you connect health apps (Apple Health, Fitbit, Google Fit):
- We only request minimal permissions (steps, sleep)
- Tokens stored locally, not on our servers
- You can disconnect at any time
- We do not access your full health history

### OAuth Providers
- Google (for login)
- Apple (for login - future)
- These providers have their own privacy policies

## Children's Privacy

ASR Health Portal is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

## Data Retention

| Data Type | Retention Period |
|-----------|-----------------|
| Account info | Until account deletion |
| Meal plans | 1 year from creation |
| Nutrition logs | 1 year from entry |
| Health imports | Until manually deleted |
| Error logs | 30 days |

## Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this policy. Changes will be:
- Posted on this page
- Noted with updated date
- Significant changes emailed to users

## Contact

For privacy questions or data requests:
- Email: privacy@asrhealthportal.com
- Subject line: "Privacy Request"

## Compliance

This policy is designed to comply with:
- CCPA (California Consumer Privacy Act)
- GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation)
- HIPAA (Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act) - where applicable

**Note**: ASR Health Portal is a wellness application, not a medical device. We do not store or process Protected Health Information (PHI) as defined by HIPAA.
